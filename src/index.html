Thai Traditional Massage in Scarborough

This ancient wellness ritual, also termed as Nuad Thai or Thai yoga massage, hails from the heart of Thailand and embodies a unique avenue to holistic health. It gracefully intertwines acupressure, assisted yoga nuances, and gentle stretching, focusing on the harmonization of the body's intricate "sen lines."

Our adept therapists, with their intricate knowledge, channel their skills using hands, thumbs, and even elbows to methodically release tension, emphasizing enhanced flexibility and profound tranquility.

Specialising in Massage for Sciatica

At A Touch of Thai, we understand that lower back pain, especially sciatica, can be a crippling concern for many. This discomfort often arises from undue pressure on the sciatic nerve, a critical nerve pathway running from your lower back down each leg. Our expertly curated massage treatments not only target the physical root causes of this pain but also usher in a sense of holistic wellbeing.

Our team is proficient in various massage techniques, from Deep Tissue to Swedish Massage, each aiming to relieve muscle tightness, reduce pressure on the nerve, and foster relaxation.
